The Honeypot Shield
Slide 2
How We Neutralize Adversarial Profiles & Resume Keyword Stuffing
  • The Threat: Synthetic datasets contain keyword stuffers (e.g. Marketing Managers listing RAG, vector databases) and intentional honeypot profiles to disqualify naive search systems.
  • Honeypot Penalty: Capped at 0.05 max score. This automatically disqualifies them from the top 100, ensuring 0% honeypot leakage.
  • Outsourcing Penalty: Identifies BPO/Consulting firms (Infosys, TCS, Genpact, Accenture, Deloitte, etc.) and reduces career quality score by 50% to prioritize product engineering pedigree.
🛡️ 5-Layer Shield Rules
✓
Impossible Tenure: Total months in history exceeds claimed years of experience * 12 + 18.
✓
Expert Zero Duration: Skills marked as "Expert" but duration is 0 months.
✓
Title-Skill Mismatch: Non-technical titles (HR, Admin) claiming >5 core AI skills.
✓
Suspicious Perfection: Claiming "Expert" for every single skill (8+ skills).
✓
YoE vs Skills: Low experience (<1 YoE) claiming >15 skills.

Multi-Signal Scoring System
Slide 4
Calibrated Weights to Align with a Startup Founding AI Engineer Profile
Score = (SkillMatch × 0.40) + (CareerQuality × 0.25) + (Behavioral × 0.20) + (Availability × 0.10) + (Location × 0.05) - Disqualifiers + EducationBonus
Skill Match
40%

TF-IDF similarity (45%), hard-skill count (35%), assessments (10%), GitHub (10%). Proficiency multipliers: Expert 3x, Advanced 2x.

Career Quality
25%

Ratio of experience at product vs consulting companies. Tracks applied ML tenure. Penalizes job-hopping (avg tenure < 18m).

Behavioral
20%

Response rate (40%), recency of activity (35%), open-to-work bonus (10%), interview completion (15%). Recency denominator set to 180d.

Availability
10%

Notice period score: ≤30d gets maximum score (1.0). Progressively penalized up to 90d notice (0.3) or more (0.1).

Location & Edu
5% + Bonus

Location fit prioritizes Pune/Noida or relocation in India. Education bonus adds up to 0.4 for Tier 1 CS/ML degrees.

The Validation & Quality Audit
Slide 5
Ensuring 100% Compliant Format, Zero Tied Ranks, and High Top-10 NDCG
  • NDCG Alignment: Half the score is decided by NDCG@10. Our scoring focuses heavily on ranking top product engineers (Zomato, Sarvam AI, Razorpay) directly in the top 10.
  • Zero Tied Scores: We introduced a micro-tiebreaker (GitHub, response rate, profile completeness scaled to 1e-6) to ensure no candidate ranks are tied at 4 decimal places, avoiding arbitrary sorting.
  • Strict Verification: The submission file complies fully with the official validate_submission.py script (schema, rank sequence, non-increasing score, and lexicographical backup rules).
